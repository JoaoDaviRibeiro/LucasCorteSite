When Litigation Is Inevitable: Building a Defensible Evidence Timeline

Litigation readiness starts early. This approach helps legal teams preserve evidence, align stakeholders, and defend key facts with clarity.

Litigation May 7, 2026 6 min read

Executive Summary

Litigation timelines can quickly become contested. A defensible evidence timeline aligns facts, mitigates spoliation risk, and supports consistent testimony.

Preservation and Legal Hold

Implement a litigation hold with clear custodians and data sources. Track compliance and retain audit logs to demonstrate good-faith preservation.

  • Identify critical repositories and communications tools.
  • Document hold notices and acknowledgement receipts.
  • Coordinate with IT to prevent auto-deletion policies.

Timeline Architecture

Build a master timeline anchored to documents, contracts, and communications. Validate date integrity and note inconsistencies early.

Witness Coordination

Prepare key witnesses with a shared timeline and approved narrative. Consistency and accuracy are essential to credibility.
