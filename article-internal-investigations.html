Internal Investigations: Preserving Privilege and Decision Integrity

Sensitive investigations require disciplined legal oversight. This guide outlines protocols that protect privilege and support governance outcomes.

Compliance April 29, 2026 8 min read

Executive Summary

Investigations carry reputational and regulatory exposure. Clear protocols protect privilege, document integrity, and internal trust.

Privilege Framework

Define counsel roles, reporting structures, and documentation rules at the outset. Limit distribution to preserve attorney-client privilege.

  • Designate a single legal point of contact.
  • Use clear privilege markings on work product.
  • Separate investigation notes from operational files.

Investigation Governance

Establish timelines, interview protocols, and decision criteria. Maintain audit-ready documentation in case of regulatory inquiry.

Communication Discipline

Coordinate internal messaging with legal guidance to avoid inconsistent narratives. Document response plans for external inquiries.
