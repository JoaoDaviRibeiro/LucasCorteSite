Commercial Leasing in 2026: Clauses That Matter in Volatile Markets

Commercial lease structures need resilience. This guide highlights the clauses that protect revenue and operational flexibility.

Real Estate May 5, 2026 5 min read

Executive Summary

Lease negotiations are more complex in unpredictable markets. The right clauses can protect against inflation risk, tenant disruption, and operational uncertainty.

Escalation and Indexing

CPI-linked escalation clauses preserve value while keeping tenants engaged. Define clear thresholds and recalculation cadence.

  • Set escalation bands to avoid extreme volatility.
  • Clarify base rent adjustments and cap exposure.
  • Confirm audit rights for pass-through expenses.

Force Majeure and Remedy Provisions

Define triggers for rent relief and performance obligations. Avoid broad carve-outs that erode enforceability.

Assignment and Subleasing

Balance landlord control with tenant flexibility. Establish approval criteria and transfer notice timelines.
