Settlement Strategy: Quantifying Litigation Risk for Executive Decisions

Settlement decisions require more than intuition. This framework helps executives quantify risk, cost, and reputational exposure.

Litigation April 25, 2026 6 min read

Executive Summary

A structured settlement analysis weighs downside exposure, legal fees, and strategic priorities. Quantification supports disciplined decision-making.

Scenario Modeling

Build three outcomes: conservative, expected, and aggressive. Attach probability weights based on evidence strength and jurisdictional considerations.

  • Quantify defense costs through key milestones.
  • Include reputational and operational disruptions.
  • Coordinate with insurance carriers and reserves.

Negotiation Windows

Identify favorable settlement windows around discovery, dispositive motions, and mediation. Timing can materially affect leverage.

Board Reporting

Provide concise, data-backed updates to decision makers. Ensure confidentiality and privilege protections remain in place.
